How It Works:

  1. Enter your Telegram username (required)
  2. Enter a valid Mega link in the input field
  3. If your link is encrypted, provide the decryption key (optional)
  4. Click the "Trade" button to submit your link
  5. Your link will be validated and sent to our system
  6. After validation, you'll receive a different Mega link on Telegram
  7. The exchange happens within 24 hours

Note: Only valid Mega links are accepted. Spam or invalid links will be rejected.
